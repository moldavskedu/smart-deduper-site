Smart Deduper

Google Sheets add-on

Fast, safe duplicate cleanup. Keeps one row per group, marks the rest for review. Works across selected sheets, respects name/ID differences, and never deletes without your confirmation.

Install from Google Workspace Marketplace

When your listing is live, update the button link.

Key features

  • Smart IDs: Email / Phone / Customer ID / Serial
  • “Always keep one” enforcement with clear coloring
  • Preview & Logs: pop-outs, reset colors, advanced options
  • No surprise deletions: nothing removed unless you confirm

Privacy-friendly

  • Runs entirely in your Google account
  • Scopes: script.container.ui, spreadsheets.currentonly
